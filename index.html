About Us

 

We're a student-led start-up, mentored by Carl Heinse. Our mission is to create a cheaper, more durable way to securely shield sea vessels from ocean pressure. We plan on doing this with our patent-pending product that is designed to endure over 6,000 PSI. Tested by researchers at Newark University.
